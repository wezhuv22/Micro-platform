About Micro-Platform

Your comprehensive single-cell analysis platform powered by cutting-edge bioinformatics tools.

Features

  • Interactive data exploration and visualization
  • Advanced clustering and cell type identification
  • Differential gene expression analysis
  • Pathway enrichment analysis
  • Trajectory analysis and pseudotime inference
  • Multi-sample comparison and batch correction

Supported Data Types

  • Single-cell RNA sequencing (scRNA-seq)
  • Single-nucleus RNA sequencing (snRNA-seq)
  • Spatial transcriptomics
  • Single-cell ATAC sequencing (scATAC-seq)
